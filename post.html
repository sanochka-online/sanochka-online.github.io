That is made to evaluate your current knowledge and to predict the result later on. I do not recommend believing them. I will send the instructions on effective and time-productive self-review to your telegram account after you complete the first listening test.

Luckily, listening is one of the most easiest and fastest skills to master and I think you will smash it. Here are some tips for first listening mock test(for all sections):
1. Do breath exercise before test(yes, it does help), here's how to do it: Вдыхаешь два раза животом(да животом, то есть когда вдыхаешь носом в диафрагму т.е увеличивается живот), затем ждешь пару секунд и медленно выдыхаешь носом или ртом(уменьшая живот)
2. Things and words are almost always paraphrased, so do not look at exact words, but look at the meaning of the sentences.
3. When there is silence before sections I recommend looking at blank spaces and trying to determine what type of word should be there(numbers or verb/noun/adjective or maybe name of someplace).
4. You might go through questions fast and highlight keywords, which seem important to you.
